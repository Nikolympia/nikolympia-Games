Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nikolympia Games ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website and play our games (the "Site").

By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Usernames: When you play our games, you may enter a display name for leaderboards. This is stored locally in your browser and may be submitted to our leaderboard backend (typically Google Firebase; see Section 1.3).
  • Contact Information: If you contact us through our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message content.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Game Data: Your game scores, survival time, levels reached, and other gameplay metrics may be collected for global leaderboards.
  • Local Storage: We use your browser's localStorage to save game progress, settings, unlocked content, and upgrade purchases. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers (except leaderboard scores).
  • Log Data: When you visit the Site, our servers may automatically collect standard log information including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access the Site, including the device type, screen resolution, and whether you are using a mobile or desktop browser.

1.3 Google Firebase (authentication and cloud data)

If you use optional sign-in (for example Google or email through Firebase Authentication), Google processes authentication data according to Google’s Privacy Policy. Identifiers may include your account email, a unique user ID, and sign-in metadata.

We use Google Cloud Firestore (Firebase) to store account-related information you generate on the Site, such as display name, avatar choice, liked games, play statistics, and cloud-synced game save data where a game supports it. Leaderboard entries you submit may also be stored in our Firebase database (display name and score-related fields).

You can sign out, clear local browser data, and contact us to request deletion of cloud data associated with your account where applicable.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

2.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the site owners.

2.2 How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: We use localStorage (similar to cookies) to save your game progress and preferences. Without these, you would lose your progress every time you close the browser.
  • Analytics: We do not currently use Google Analytics or similar third-party analytics scripts on this Site. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this Policy and provide appropriate notice and choices where required by law.
  • Advertising Cookies and similar technologies: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads, including based on your prior visits to our Site or other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising from Google by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your region and settings. Where the law requires it (for example in the EEA or UK), we will obtain consent before using cookies or similar technologies for advertising.

2.3 Third-Party Cookies

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to this Site and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices or by using the NAI opt-out tool.

3. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on our Site. Google AdSense may use the following technologies:

  • Cookies — to serve ads based on your interests and browsing behavior
  • Web beacons — to monitor ad impressions and interactions
  • DoubleClick DART Cookie — Google uses this cookie to serve ads based on your visits to this Site and other sites on the internet

For more information about how Google uses your data, please review Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data when you use our partners' sites.

We may publish an ads.txt file at the root of our domain so advertisers can verify authorized sellers, as recommended for AdSense publishers.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain our games and leaderboard functionality
  • To display your username and score on global leaderboards
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To improve the Site, game performance, and user experience
  • To serve advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To improve the Site, fix bugs, and understand broad usage trends without third-party analytics at this time
  • To prevent abuse, cheating, and unauthorized access

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with the following third parties:

  • Google Firebase / Firestore: Optional authentication, account profiles, synced preferences, and leaderboard and game-related data stored in our Firebase project (see Section 1.3).
  • Google AdSense: Serving advertisements (see Section 3).
  • Dreamlo (optional fallback): Some game builds may send leaderboard display names and scores to dreamlo.com when Firebase is unavailable (for example certain local or legacy setups).
  • FormSubmit: Processing contact form submissions.

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing how they use your information.

6. Data Retention

  • Local game data: Stored indefinitely in your browser's localStorage until you clear your browser data.
  • Leaderboard and Firebase data: Retained in Firebase (or legacy leaderboard services) for as long as those features are offered, unless you ask us to delete account-linked data where feasible.
  • Contact form data: Retained for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry, then deleted.
  • Server logs: Typically retained for up to 90 days.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your data (including leaderboard entries).
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Opt-out: Opt out of personalized advertising (see Section 2.3).
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

8. Children's Privacy

Nikolympia Games hosts general-audience games. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Users

The Site is operated from and data may be processed in various locations. By using the Site, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
