About

The short version.

Hey

I'm Nikos. I started Nikolympia because I wanted to build games people can just play. No downloading a 2GB file, no making an account, no waiting. You click a link, the game loads, and you're in.

Everything runs in the browser — desktop, phone, tablet. I build these with JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas so they're lightweight. Your progress saves in the browser so you can come back anytime.

The Games

The hub includes browser games like Void Survivors (survival roguelike with a global leaderboard), Up or Lose (vertical climber), Ascension Protocol, and more as they ship.

I'll keep adding titles over time — I'd rather take my time than rush out something bad.

Why Browser Games?

Browser games are underrated. You don't need a gaming PC. You don't need to install anything. You can play on your phone on the bus or on your computer at home. The barrier is literally zero.

Plus HTML5 Canvas can actually handle a lot more than people think. These aren't your 2010 Flash games (RIP though, those were fun).

Got Feedback?

Bug reports, game ideas, whatever — hit me up on the contact page. I read everything.
