What you’ll find here

Nikolympia Games is a free browser game arcade: open a page, play immediately, no install. We run live leaderboards so scores are real competition, not fake bots. The site is supported by advertising (Google AdSense) so the games stay free — see our Privacy Policy for how ads and cookies work.

Void Survivors

Survival roguelike: waves of enemies, evolving weapons, bosses every 10 levels. Sessions are a few minutes to much longer if you’re good. Scores post to a global board.

Up or Lose

Fast vertical climb: auto-jump, moving platforms, boosts and hazards. Short runs, high retry — great on phone or keyboard. Height-based leaderboard.

Ascension Protocol

Idle / incremental sci-fi: multiple phases, currencies, and long-term progression in the browser. For players who like spreadsheets with lore.

FAQ

Quick answers for players and for anyone reviewing how the site works.

Do I need an account?
No. Every game runs without signing in. An optional free account syncs likes and history across devices and can tie your name to leaderboards.
Where is my save data?
Progress is stored in your browser (local storage) by default. If you sign in, some data may sync through our backend (see the Privacy Policy).
Are leaderboards real?
Yes. Scores are submitted to our database (Google Firebase). We discourage cheating and can remove abusive or impossible entries.
Why are there ads?
Ads help pay for hosting and development so we can keep games free. We use Google AdSense; you can read how Google uses data in our Privacy Policy.
What about kids?
Our Terms require users to be at least 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Use the contact page or email — we read messages about bugs, ideas, and feedback.
